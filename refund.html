Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Free tier

Quantra AI offers a free plan so you can evaluate the core product before paying. No card is required for the free tier.

2. Subscriptions

Paid plans (Pro, Ultimate) are billed in advance each period (monthly unless stated). Your plan renews automatically until you cancel.

3. Cancellation

You may cancel at any time from the billing portal in your account menu. Cancellation stops future renewals; you keep paid features until the end of the current billing period. We do not provide partial-period refunds for cancellations unless required by law.

4. Refunds

If you are charged in error, or are dissatisfied within 14 days of a first subscription purchase, contact us and we will review a refund in good faith. Renewals after the first period are generally non-refundable, except where required by applicable consumer law.

5. Enterprise & pilots

Enterprise licences and paid pilots are governed by their individual written agreements, which take precedence over this policy.

6. How to request

Email hello@quantra.ai with your account email and the charge in question. We aim to respond within a few business days.
