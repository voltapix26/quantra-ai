Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What we collect

Account data: your email, name and workspace name, and a securely hashed password (we never store plain passwords). Usage data: your watchlist, settings and basic feature-usage counts. Technical data: IP address and request metadata used for security and rate-limiting. Billing data: handled by our payment processor — we store only a customer reference and plan, not card numbers.

2. How we use it

To provide and secure the Service, sync your watchlist and settings, send transactional emails (verification, password reset, billing), enforce plan limits, and meet legal obligations. We do not sell your personal data.

3. Third parties (processors)

We share data only with processors needed to run the Service, for example: a database host, an email provider (for verification/reset), a payment processor (for billing), and market-data providers (which receive only the symbols you query, not your identity). Each processes data under its own terms.

4. Cookies

We use a single essential, http-only session cookie to keep you signed in. It is not used for advertising. Disabling it will prevent sign-in.

5. Data retention & your rights

We keep your data while your account is active. You can export your data (a JSON download) or permanently delete your account at any time from the account menu — deletion removes your profile, watchlist and workspace. Depending on your jurisdiction you may also have rights to access, correct, restrict or object to processing; contact us to exercise them.

6. Security

Passwords are salted and hashed (scrypt). Sessions use http-only cookies, secured over HTTPS in production. Sensitive auth endpoints are rate-limited. No system is perfectly secure; use a strong, unique password.

7. International transfers & children

Your data may be processed in countries other than yours, subject to appropriate safeguards. The Service is not directed at children under the age required to consent in your jurisdiction.

8. Changes & contact

We may update this Policy; material changes will be notified. Questions or requests: hello@quantra.ai.
